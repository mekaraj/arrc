Our Mission

WildARRC works tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and release injured or orphaned wildlife across Bengaluru. Whether it’s a bird trapped in manjha, a grounded hatchling, or a rescued reptile, we act swiftly and with care.

How You Can Help

  • Report injured wildlife via our voice interface below.
  • Volunteer — training provided.
  • Spread awareness about preventing manjha-related injuries.
